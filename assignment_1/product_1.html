Ferrari Roma

The Ferrari Roma Spider is a grand tourer that blends timeless elegance with contemporary performance, carrying the spirit of La Dolce Vita into the modern era. It’s a car that embodies both sophistication and power — sculpted with aerodynamic precision yet finished with subtle luxury. Designed for drivers who value both emotion and refinement, the Roma Spider delivers an open-top driving experience that feels equally at home on winding coastal roads or through the heart of a city at night.

Price: $222,620

Engine and Performance

Under the hood, the Ferrari Roma Spider is powered by a 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing 612 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque. Mated to an eight-speed dual-clutch transmission derived from the SF90 Stradale, the car sprints from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.4 seconds. The engine’s linear power delivery and sharp throttle response create an effortless surge of acceleration, while the distinctive Ferrari soundtrack ensures every drive feels alive. Precision-tuned suspension and rear-wheel drive dynamics give it the perfect balance between agility and comfort, making it an ideal companion for both spirited drives and long journeys.

Design & Interior

Inside, the Roma Spider balances minimalism with opulence. The cockpit is wrapped in rich Italian leather, accented by aluminum and carbon fiber details that underscore its sporting heritage. A digital instrument cluster, intuitive infotainment system, and customizable driver interface bring modern convenience without overwhelming the aesthetic simplicity. The retractable soft-top folds seamlessly in 13.5 seconds at speeds of up to 60 km/h, preserving the car’s graceful silhouette whether the roof is up or down.

Driving Dynamics

Every element of the Roma Spider is engineered to make driving feel instinctive. Its steering is light yet communicative, offering confidence through corners, while adaptive dampers continuously adjust to road conditions to ensure a composed ride. Ferrari’s advanced electronic systems — including Side Slip Control 6.0 and dynamic traction management — allow the driver to explore the car’s limits safely and precisely. Whether cruising or carving through a mountain pass, the Roma Spider rewards smooth inputs and deliberate control, reminding you that true performance is as much about grace as it is about speed.
