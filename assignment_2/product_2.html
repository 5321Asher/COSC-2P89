Porsche 718 Cayman GT4 RS

The Porsche 718 Cayman GT4 RS represents the pinnacle of Porsche’s mid-engine engineering — a car designed with pure driving engagement in mind. It channels the spirit of the track into a road-legal machine that’s both raw and refined, offering balance, agility, and precision few cars can match. Every aspect of the GT4 RS is focused on one goal: to deliver an unfiltered connection between driver and machine. From its aerodynamics to its naturally aspirated engine, it stands as one of Porsche’s most authentic performance cars in decades.

Price: $350,000

Engine and Performance

At the heart of the 718 GT4 RS lies a 4.0-liter naturally aspirated flat-six engine borrowed from the 911 GT3. It produces 493 horsepower and 331 lb-ft of torque, revving to an exhilarating 9,000 rpm. Paired exclusively with a lightning-fast 7-speed PDK transmission, it rockets from 0 to 100 km/h in 3.4 seconds and reaches a top speed of 315 km/h. The soundtrack is pure motorsport — a sharp, metallic wail that builds to a crescendo through the roof-mounted air intakes. Every shift feels immediate, every throttle input alive. It’s not just powerful — it’s visceral, designed to make you feel the engineering at work beneath your hands.

Design & Interior

Inside, the 718 GT4 RS blends functional simplicity with lightweight luxury. The cabin is dominated by Alcantara, carbon fiber, and exposed stitching that reflect its racing pedigree. Weight-saving door pulls and minimalist switchgear reduce distractions, while deeply bolstered seats keep the driver firmly in place through aggressive cornering. Despite its focus on performance, Porsche’s craftsmanship remains impeccable — every surface feels deliberate, every control precisely weighted. The steering wheel and gear paddles are wrapped in tactile materials that reinforce the car’s driver-first philosophy.

Driving Dynamics

The GT4 RS delivers a driving experience that’s as communicative as it is intense. The steering is razor-sharp, giving a direct sense of what each tire is doing. Porsche’s suspension tuning keeps the chassis planted and predictable, even under extreme lateral loads. Carbon-ceramic brakes provide immense stopping power and confidence lap after lap. It’s a car that rewards precision and punishes hesitation — every corner becomes a conversation between driver, grip, and gravity. Yet despite its ferocity, it remains usable on real roads, offering enough compliance to make every drive feel like a special event.
