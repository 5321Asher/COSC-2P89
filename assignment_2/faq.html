Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Virgona Racing's return policy?

At Virgona Racing, we offer a 7-day return policy on all vehicles, provided they are returned in the same condition as purchased and with all original documentation.

2. Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we have partnerships with several financial institutions to provide competitive financing options for our customers. Please contact our sales team for more details.

3. Can I schedule a test drive?

Absolutely! We encourage potential buyers to schedule a test drive to experience the performance and handling of our vehicles. Please contact us to arrange a convenient time.

4. Are your vehicles certified pre-owned?

Yes, all our pre-owned vehicles undergo a rigorous inspection and certification process to ensure they meet our high standards for quality and performance.

5. Do you provide vehicle history reports?

Yes, we provide comprehensive vehicle history reports for all our pre-owned vehicles, giving you peace of mind about your purchase.
